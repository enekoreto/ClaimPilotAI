# Fictional Home Insurance Policy

HOM-201: Escape of Water
We cover sudden and accidental water damage from fixed domestic installations, appliances, or internal pipes. Photos, repair quote, cause report, and invoice are required. Gradual leakage and poor maintenance are excluded.

HOM-202: Storm and Weather Damage
We cover damage to the insured building caused directly by storm, hail, lightning, or heavy rainfall where the home was adequately maintained. Photos and contractor repair quote are required.

HOM-203: Theft and Burglary
We cover stolen household contents following forced entry or threatened violence. A police report, inventory of stolen items, and proof of ownership are required.

HOM-299: Home General Exclusions
We do not cover wear and tear, gradual deterioration, defective workmanship, or damage that existed before the policy started.
