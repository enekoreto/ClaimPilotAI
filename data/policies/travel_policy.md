# Fictional Travel Insurance Policy

TRV-101: Baggage Delay and Loss
We cover reasonable replacement costs for essential items when checked baggage is delayed by more than 12 hours or permanently lost by the carrier. The claimant must provide the carrier report, travel booking, receipts or invoices, and proof of ownership where available.

TRV-102: Trip Cancellation and Curtailment
We cover non-refundable travel and accommodation costs when cancellation is caused by serious illness, accident, death of a close family member, or official travel disruption. Booking confirmation and cancellation evidence are required.

TRV-103: Emergency Medical Expenses Abroad
We cover necessary emergency medical treatment outside the country of residence during an insured trip. A medical certificate and itemised invoice must be provided. Pre-existing conditions are excluded unless explicitly accepted.

TRV-199: General Exclusions
We do not cover losses caused by unattended belongings in public areas, alcohol or drug misuse, intentional acts, or costs that can be recovered from an airline, hotel, card provider, or other insurer.
