# Fictional Car Insurance Policy

CAR-301: Collision Damage
We cover accidental collision damage to the insured vehicle subject to the policy excess. Photos, repair quote or garage estimate, and incident description are required.

CAR-302: Theft or Attempted Theft
We cover vehicle theft, attempted theft, and theft-related damage where reasonable security measures were taken. A police report and proof of ownership are required.

CAR-303: Windscreen and Glass
We cover accidental windscreen, window, and glass damage. A repair invoice or garage estimate is required.

CAR-399: Motor General Exclusions
We do not cover driving under the influence, unlicensed drivers, deliberate damage, racing, or use outside the declared policy terms.
